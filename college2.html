About Us

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE), located in Koovappally, Kottayam, is one of Kerala's premier engineering institutions. Established in 2001 and managed by the Catholic Diocese of Kanjirappally, the college is known for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and strong industry connections.
